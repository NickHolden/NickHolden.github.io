Travel

One of my favorite things in life is travelling and experiencing different cultures.


In Winter 2018 I took my first international trip with my girlfriend. We visited the Great Wall of China, experienced the culture of Tokyo, Japan, went scuba diving in the Great Barrier Reef, saw the Sydney New Years Eve fireworks display, and cage dived with sharks in Port Lincoln, Australia.
